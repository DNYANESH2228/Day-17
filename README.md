---

What I Learned

1. What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information like passwords, OTPs, or bank details by pretending to be a trusted source.

---

2. Types of Phishing Attacks

- Email Phishing – Fake emails pretending to be from banks, companies, etc.
- Spear Phishing – Targeted attacks on specific individuals
- Smishing – Phishing through SMS messages
- Vishing – Phishing through phone calls

---

3. Common Signs of Phishing

- Suspicious email addresses
- Urgent or threatening messages
- Unknown links or attachments
- Spelling and grammar mistakes
- Requests for sensitive information

---

4. How Phishing Works

1. Attacker creates a fake message
2. Sends it to the victim
3. Victim clicks a link or downloads file
4. Sensitive data is stolen or malware is installed

---

5. Prevention Methods

- Do not click unknown links
- Verify sender identity
- Use strong passwords
- Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA)
- Keep software updated
